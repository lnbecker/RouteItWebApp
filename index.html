Reasons to sign up for RouteIt

Plan your routes

With RouteIt, you can not only look at thousands of routes through the city of your choice, but also create your own! Map out your route before your vacation to make sure you see everything you want to see in the most efficient way possible.

Discover more than you imagined

RouteIt helps you explore more than what you planned. With options that allow you to select the most time-efficient, scenic, and popular routes, you may find your destination has even more to see than you originally thought. Sign up to start finding new ways to discover more on your next trip!

Explore your favorite places

With users adding more routes every day, you are sure to find a walking route of your favorite city! If you don't find the city you're looking for, contact us and request a route to be added. We will add it to our list and send you travel resources in the meantime.
